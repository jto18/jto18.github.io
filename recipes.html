BREAKFAST

Oatmeal Pancakes

Ingredients

2 cups of old fashioned oats

1 1/4 cups of organic milk

1 large ripe, organic banana

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1 tablespoon of honey

1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

1 large organic egg

coconut oil for cooking

Instructions

Place all ingredients, except for egg and butter in a blender and blend until smooth. Pour in a bowl an mix with the egg until egg is fully incorporated.

Heat a small pan over medium heat. When hot, pour the coconut oil onto the pan,

using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides (about 2-3 minutes per side) and serve hot with maple syrup and fruits.

Turkey Provolone Wrap with Avocado Mayo

Ingredients

1 large sun-dried tomato tortilla shell

3 slices deli turkey meat

1 slice provolone cheese

2 tomato slices

2 lettuce leaves

For the avocado mayonnaise:

1/2 avocado

2-3 tablespoons mayonnaise

Instructions

In a small bowl, mash avocado with fork. Add in mayonnaise until it reaches your desired consistency.Set aside.

Assemble wrap: layer tortilla with mayo, turkey, cheese, tomato, and lettuce. Wrap and serve immediately, or refrigerate until ready to serve.

VEGGIE FRIED RICE

Ingredients

2 tablespoons sesame oil

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 1-inch piece fresh ginger, grated

3 eggs

4 cups brown rice (cooked, cooled, and separated)

1 cup frozen corn

1-2 cups frozen peas

1/2 tablespoon rice vinegar

2-3 tablespoons soy sauce

fresh chives, fresh basil, baby spinach, any other add ins you want!

Instructions

Heat one tablespoon oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the garlic cloves and ginger and stir fry for one minute.

Crack the eggs directly into the pan and gently push them around in the pan until barely cooked, 1-2 minutes.

Add the rice and stir fry for a few minutes, adding the other tablespoon of oil and turning the heat up to get it sizzlin. The eggs should sort of incorporate and stick to the rice.

Add the frozen corn, peas, rice vinegar, and soy sauce and stir fry for another 1-2 minutes. Remove from heat, stir in any additional add-ins (basil is a must! SO good) and serve!

PERUVIAN DISH

CEVICHE

Ingredients

1 1/4 pounds fish fillets (sea bass, sole, or flounder), cut on the bias into 1-inch dice.

1 red onion, cut in 1/2 lengthwise and sliced thin.

1 aji amarillo, rib and seeds removed, diced.

1 tbsp os Rocoto(peruvian chili)

1 clove garlic.

3/4 cup lime juice

ginger

1 tbsp of evaporated milk

salt and pepper to taste

1/2 cup cilantro leaves, chopped

1 orange sweet potato, boiled, peeled, and sliced

4 lettuce leaves

2 ears of corn, peruvian corn if you have it

peruvian cancha if you have

Instructions

In separate pots, boil the corn and the sweet potato until tender. Peel the sweet potato. Let both cool, then cut corn into 2-inch sections and sweet potato into 1-inch sections.

Place cubed raw fish in a bowl with salt and pepper, garlic, aji amarillo, rocoto.

Add enough lemon juice to cover the fish and 1tbsp of evaporated milk, add some onions and some cilantro.

Season more with salt if needed. Let marinate for 10minutes, until the fish is milky white.

For each serving, place a mound of the fish on a leaf of lettuce. Top with onions and cilantro, and add corn and sweet potato and cancha.

Enjoy it!
