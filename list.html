Peruvian Corner

This a list for the easy ingredients that don't take too long to cook or the ones that you can make in your free day and storage for a week.

  • Your favorite fruits, Don't be afraid to try new things

    • Fresh in season or frozen,organic when possible

    Consume: 3-4 times a day

  • Dark chocolate in bar or powder

    • Consume with moderation

  • Fish & Shellfish(wild alaskan salmon,alaskan black cod, sardines)

    • Consume: 2-6 times a week

  • Vegetables

    • Choose from all parts of the color spectrum,organic when possible

    Consume: 4-5 times a day minimum

  • Pasta

    • Al dente, Whole grain if you can

    Consume: 2-3 times a week

  • Whole-Soy foods

    • edemame,soy nuts,soymilk, tofu

    Consume: 1-2 times a day

  • Proteins

    • Natural cheeses, yogurt(plain or greek),omega-3 enriched eggs, skinless poultry,lean meats

    Consume: 1-2 times a week minimum

  • Healthy fats

    • extra-virgin olive oil,coconut oil, nuts, avocados,seeds

    Consume: 5-7 times a day

  • Beans & Legumes

    • Consume: 1-2 times a day

  • Whole and cracked grains

    • Rice (any color), Barley, Maize, Rye, Oats,Quinoa,cereals

    Consume:3-5 times a day

  • Cooked asian Mushrooms

  • Healthy Herbs & Spices

    • Garlc,ginger, tumeric,cinnamon

  • Green tea, Oolong tea
