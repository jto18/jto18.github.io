Beet cake

Time:30 min Serving: 8

Ingredients

2 cooked beet,peel and cut

3 medium eggs

1 1/2 cup of greek yogurt

1/4 cup of honey

1 1/2 cup of whole wheat flour

1/2 cup of unsweetened powder cacao

1 tsp baking powder

coconut oil

Instructions

Heat oven to 350 degrees F, grease the baking pan, blend beets then in a bowl mix the eggs with the beets, yogurt and honey, add the flour, cacao powder and baking powder.

Continue mixing if you wish you can add 1/4 cup of brown sugar, walnuts pour the mix in the baking pan and put in the oven for 30 min or until a pick comes out clean.

This a healthy version of the chocolate cake

Homemade strawberry Ice-Cream

Time:10 min Servings:4

Ingredients

Cashews, 1 handful

Frozen Strawberries, 1 1/2 handful

Vanilla extract, 1/2 tsp

Instructions

Blend all the ingredients with water, It should be frozen enough that you can turn the blender upside down and none falls out
