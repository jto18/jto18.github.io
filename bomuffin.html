Banana oatmeal muffin with chocolate chip

Time:15min Servings:10

Ingredients

1/2 cups old-fashioned oats

1/2 Whole wheat flour

2 overripe bananas

2 large eggs

1/4 cup of honey or maple syrup

1tsp of cinnamon

chocolate chips 70% organic cacao

1 tsp baking soda

1/4 of coconut oil

Instructions

Pre-heat oven at 350 degrees F, lightly grease muffin cups with some coconut oil, mash the bananas well in a separate bowl,
then in another bowl mix in this order: the eggs, coconut oil, baking soda, honey and cinnamon, mashed bananas,flour and oats and finally the chocolate chips.

Pour the mixture into the muffin cups amd bake for 15 min.

When they are done let it cool and put in a glass container and then you have a healthy breakfast or snack to grab and go.
