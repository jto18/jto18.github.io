OATMEAL COOKIES sugar free

Ingredients

1 apple or applesauce

2 banana

1tsp of honey

2 cups of oatmeal

1 tsp of baking soda

coconut oil

1/4 almond milk

1 tsp vanilla extract

1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

for toppings:raisins, cramberries or any dried fruit, pecan, walnuts or other nuts, flaked coconut, chocolate chips.

Instructions

preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease the baking sheet with coconut oil.

In a large bowl mix oats, almond milk, smashed bananas, blended apple,baking soda,cinnamon and vanilla extract

Mix with any tops that you want. Drop the mix by rounded teaspoons in the baking sheet. Bake for 20 min.
