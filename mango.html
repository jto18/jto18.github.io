Mango Smoothie

Ingredients

2 1/2 frozen mango chunks

1 1/2 cup of greek yogurt

1 tbsp of honey

juice from half lime

1/4 tsp of organic instant powder cacao

1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

Toss ingredients into blender except for the cacao powder and cinnamon you can choose between the cinnamon or cacao powder to sprinkle.

*If you like cinnamon you can blend it with all the ingredients
